# Fog-Breaker-Burst
Fog-Breaker Burst ⚡💨
Fog-Breaker Burst is an elite utility exclusively designed for Blox Fruits. This tool focuses on maximizing combat potential and environmental clarity, allowing players to dominate the toughest challenges in the game. 
🛠️ Key Features
• Extreme Transformation Synergy: Specifically engineered for the Buddha transformation. Transforming into a Buddha significantly extends your combat reach, and using specific character skins will further enhance your overall range. 
• Sea Raid Visibility (Danger Level 6): Even in the middle of a Danger Level 6 Sea Raid, this tool ensures perfect visibility. It instantly purges heavy atmospheric effects like fog and clouds via .Destroy(), guaranteeing a clear line of sight through the storm. 
• The Golden Ratio Execution: Operates with high-precision timing at 0.045-second intervals. This ensures a seamless flow of interaction with the server without triggering safety kicks. 
• Ultra-Lightweight Environment: By removing dense atmosphere objects, the script maintains a high-performance environment, allowing you to see across the entire map without lag.
